What is Solana

Solana is a high-performance, open-source blockchain platform designed to support scalable and user-friendly applications. It aims to provide fast transaction speeds and low fees by utilizing a unique combination of proof-of-stake (PoS) and proof-of-history (PoH) consensus mechanisms. Solana's architecture allows it to process thousands of transactions per second, making it suitable for decentralized finance (DeFi), non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and other decentralized applications (dApps). The platform's native cryptocurrency is called SOL, which is used for transaction fees and staking within the network.
